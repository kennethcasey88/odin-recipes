Spaghetti

Easy pasta recipe:

Quick recipe that doesn't require many recipes or much effort.

Ingredients:

  • Ground meat
  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. Start boil water for the spaghetti
  2. Fry the meat
  3. Add chopped onions
  4. Add tomato sauce
  5. Press in some garlic
  6. Throw spaghetti in the boiling water
  7. Combine the meat sauce and spaghetti and enjoy!
